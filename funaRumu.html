Account Deletion Instructions

How to permanently delete your account

Hello User,

You've requested information on how to delete your Funa Rumu account. Please follow the steps below carefully to permanently remove your account and data.

1
Log into Your Account
Open the Funa Rumu app and sign in to your account using your credentials.
2
Navigate to Profile Settings
Tap on your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select "Profile" from the menu.
3
Find Account Management
Scroll down to the "User Actions" section where you'll find the account deletion option.
4
Initiate Deletion Process
Tap on "Delete Account" button. You'll be asked to confirm this action in a pop-up window.
5
Confirm Deletion
Read the warning carefully, then confirm your decision by tapping "Delete Account" in the confirmation dialog.
